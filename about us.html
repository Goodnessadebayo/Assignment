Privacy Policy

We collect the following types of information:
  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email, phone number, address, etc.
  • Financial Information: Bank details, transaction history, etc.
  • Identification Verification Information: Government-issued IDs for KYC/AML compliance.
  • Trading and Transactional Information: Trade history, trade amounts, etc.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, cookies, and log data.

We use your information to:
  • Manage your account and execute trades.
  • Comply with regulatory requirements like KYC and AML.
  • Prevent fraud and illegal activities.
  • Improve website functionality and security.
  • Communicate updates and promotions to you.
  • Provide personalized recommendations.

We may share your information with:
  • Service Providers: For transaction processing and KYC/AML compliance.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or legal requests.
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers or acquisitions.

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit the functionality of some website features.

We use encryption and secure data storage to protect your information. However, no data transmission method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:
  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with the updated effective date.
