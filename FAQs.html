Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find answers to common questions about our platform, services, and processes.

Quîlla is a stock trading platform and online marketplace for vendors to advertise and sell their goods. We offer a seamless experience for both stock traders and sellers to connect with a wide audience. You can trade stocks, buy products, and set up your store as a vendor.

To become a vendor on our platform, simply visit the Vendor Registration Page, create an account, and list your products or services. We provide all the necessary tools to help you set up and start selling in no time.

Yes, we have a competitive fee structure for vendors. Our pricing depends on the type of products or services you sell. Visit our Vendor Pricing Page for more detailed information about the fees and how they apply to your sales.

Your security is our top priority. Our platform uses advanced encryption and security protocols to protect both your stock trades and online transactions. We comply with industry standards to ensure all data and financial information are securely handled. For more information, visit our Security Policy.

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit/debit cards, and direct bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through trusted payment gateways. Please visit our Payment Methods Page for more details on accepted payment options.

We’re here to help! You can contact our customer support team via email at help@Quilla.com or visit our Contact Us Page for additional support options, including live chat and phone support.
